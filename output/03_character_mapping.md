### 1. Yudhishthira → **Yash Kaurav**

**ORIGINAL → NEW**: Yudhishthira → Yash Kaurav, the Idealistic CEO with a troubled past.
  
**NEW IDENTITY**: Yash Kaurav is the CEO of a struggling non-profit organization dedicated to community welfare in the Suburban Compound of San Hastina. He is a former corporate lawyer who left a lucrative career to pursue his idealistic vision of justice and righteousness, often clashing with the corrupt practices of the Kuru Corporation.

**PRESERVED ESSENCE**: Yash’s core conflict revolves around his commitment to truth and righteousness, which often puts him at odds with the ruthless power dynamics of the city. His motivation stems from a deep desire to reclaim his family's legacy while battling his addiction to gambling, which he struggles to overcome.

**TRANSFORMED DETAILS**: In this context, Yash's gambling addiction is represented through risky investments in social causes that often backfire, mirroring the dice game from the original story. His rigid adherence to ethics often leads to personal and professional setbacks, making him a tragic figure in a world dominated by corruption.

**SIGNATURE TRAITS**: Yash has disheveled hair, signifying his idealism and the toll of sleepless nights spent strategizing. He wears tailored suits that are slightly rumpled, showcasing his commitment to his cause over personal appearance. He carries a vintage pocket watch, a family heirloom that symbolizes the weight of his family's expectations. His speech is laced with earnestness, often quoting philosophical texts and ethical dilemmas.

**KEY RELATIONSHIPS**: Yash is often at odds with Duryodhana (his rival CEO) and has a strained friendship with Krishna, who tries to guide him through the moral complexities of the corporate world. He frequently confides in Draupadi, who challenges him to take more decisive action against the Kauravas.

**CHARACTER VOICE**: “In a city where truth is a currency few can afford, I’d rather be broke than sell my soul.”

### 2. Arjuna → **Ari Kaurav**

**ORIGINAL → NEW**: Arjuna → Ari Kaurav, The Reluctant Vigilante.

**NEW IDENTITY**: Ari Kaurav is a skilled hacker and vigilante operating out of The Gully. He is a member of the Pandava faction, torn between his loyalty to his family and his moral compass, constantly confronted by the criminality of the city. He uses his skills to expose corruption within the Kuru Corporation.

**PRESERVED ESSENCE**: Ari’s internal conflict echoes Arjuna's moral crisis. He struggles to reconcile his duty as a vigilante with his familial ties to the Kauravas, particularly his love for his cousin Duryodhana. His reluctance to engage in violence stems from a deep-seated empathy for the victims of crime.

**TRANSFORMED DETAILS**: The moral crisis is now represented through hacking into corporate systems and exposing secrets rather than traditional combat. The philosophical discourse is portrayed through his interactions with his hacker group, where they debate the ethics of their actions.

**SIGNATURE TRAITS**: Ari has a punk aesthetic with dyed hair and piercings, reflecting his rebellious nature. He wears a hoodie and cargo pants, always moving with a sense of urgency. His weapon of choice is a custom-made EMP device that can disable security systems, making him an elusive target. He often speaks in tech jargon mixed with street slang.

**KEY RELATIONSHIPS**: Ari’s relationship with Yash is one of camaraderie but also tension, as Yash pushes for non-violent means of justice. His relationship with Duryodhana is complicated, as he feels both loyalty and love for him while disapproving of his ruthless tactics.

**CHARACTER VOICE**: “You want me to fight? I’m not ready to take lives, but exposing the truth? Now that’s a battle I can win.”

### 3. Bhima → **Brock ‘The Beast’ Pandava**

**ORIGINAL → NEW**: Bhima → Brock ‘The Beast’ Pandava, The Enforcer.

**NEW IDENTITY**: Brock is the muscle of the Pandava faction, known for his brute strength and fierce loyalty. He operates out of The Docks, where he runs underground fights to raise money for their cause. His reputation as a heavy-hitter makes him both respected and feared.

**PRESERVED ESSENCE**: Brock embodies Bhima's rage and thirst for vengeance, particularly against the Kauravas for Draupadi’s humiliation. He is driven by a personal code of honor, believing that strength must be used to protect the vulnerable.

**TRANSFORMED DETAILS**: His rage manifests in underground fighting rings where he seeks retribution against those who exploit the weak. The cycle of violence is highlighted through his struggle to balance loyalty with the consequences of his actions.

**SIGNATURE TRAITS**: Brock has a muscular build, tattoos depicting his family crest, and often wears combat boots and leather jackets. His weapon of choice is a heavy steel pipe, symbolic of his brutal approach to justice. He speaks in a gruff tone, often punctuating his sentences with threats or bravado.

**KEY RELATIONSHIPS**: Brock has a brotherly bond with Yash, often protecting him from the harsh realities of their world. His relationship with Draupadi is complex; he views her as a sister and is fiercely protective of her honor, leading to his vengeful actions against the Kauravas.

**CHARACTER VOICE**: “You don’t want to see me angry. I’ll break more than bones if it means protecting what’s ours.”

### 4. Draupadi → **Dahlia ‘The Phoenix’**

**ORIGINAL → NEW**: Draupadi → Dahlia ‘The Phoenix’, The Voice of Justice.

**NEW IDENTITY**: Dahlia is the fierce leader of the Draupadi faction, known for her activism in the Heights. She runs a successful nightclub that serves as a front for her philanthropic efforts, empowering women and fighting against the patriarchal oppression in San Hastina.

**PRESERVED ESSENCE**: Dahlia’s arc reflects Draupadi’s transformation from victim to avenger. Her humiliation becomes a rallying cry for her community, fueling her passion for justice while grappling with the cycle of violence and revenge.

**TRANSFORMED DETAILS**: The public humiliation in the court is mirrored by an incident at her nightclub where she is disrespected by a powerful patron, igniting her quest for justice for all women. She vows to bring down the Kauravas through strategic social movements rather than outright violence.

**SIGNATURE TRAITS**: Dahlia has striking features, often adorned with bold makeup and stylish attire that reflects her vibrant personality. She carries a customized microphone as her weapon, using her voice to inspire and incite change. Her speech is passionate, filled with rhetoric that motivates her followers.

**KEY RELATIONSHIPS**: Dahlia’s friendship with Yash is built on mutual respect and shared goals, while her relationship with Brock is one of shared pain and protection, as she often grounds him when his rage threatens to overwhelm him.

**CHARACTER VOICE**: “They think they can silence me, but I will rise from the ashes every time. Justice isn’t a choice; it’s my destiny.”

### 5. Duryodhana → **Duke Kaurav**

**ORIGINAL → NEW**: Duryodhana → Duke Kaurav, The Ruthless Corporate Titan.

**NEW IDENTITY**: Duke Kaurav is the CEO of the Kuru Corporation, known for his cunning and manipulative strategies. He is the face of corporate power in San Hastina, using his wealth and influence to control the Downtown District while eliminating any threats to his dominance.

**PRESERVED ESSENCE**: Duke’s jealousy and ambition remain intact, driving him to crush the Pandavas and maintain his grip on power. His refusal to yield to anyone reflects his deep-seated entitlement and thirst for control.

**TRANSFORMED DETAILS**: The rigged dice game is represented through corporate takeovers and hostile business tactics, where Duke will stop at nothing to expand his empire. His entitlement is reflected in his interactions with both allies and enemies.

**SIGNATURE TRAITS**: Duke dresses impeccably in sharp suits, always exuding confidence and power. He has a signature luxury sports car that embodies his status. He speaks with a charismatic yet threatening tone, often using charm to mask his ruthlessness.

**KEY RELATIONSHIPS**: Duke sees Ari as a threat and is constantly plotting against him, while he views Yash as an obstacle to his corporate ambitions. His relationship with Shakuni is one of mutual manipulation, as they scheme to eliminate their enemies.

**CHARACTER VOICE**: “In this city, power is the only currency that matters. And I have more than enough to buy loyalty or destroy those who oppose me.”

**PRESERVED ESSENCE**: Kaden’s internal conflict mirrors that of Karna, as he grapples with his loyalty to Duke while seeking recognition and acceptance from the Pandavas. His tragic loyalty leads him to make choices that ultimately doom him.

**TRANSFORMED DETAILS**: Kaden’s loyalty to Duke is juxtaposed with moments of doubt, especially when he witnesses the injustices inflicted on the innocent by the Kauravas. His struggle for identity is reflected in his attempts to prove himself while hiding his lineage.

**SIGNATURE TRAITS**: Kaden has a rugged appearance with scars from his battles, wearing tactical gear that reflects his role as an enforcer. His weapon of choice is a customized crossbow that symbolizes his precision and skill. He often speaks with a mix of bravado and vulnerability, reflecting his inner turmoil.

**KEY RELATIONSHIPS**: Kaden has a complicated relationship with Duke, who exploits his loyalty, while he harbors unexpressed feelings for Dahlia, complicating his loyalties further. His internal conflict intensifies as he learns more about his past.

**CHARACTER VOICE**: “Loyalty is a double-edged sword. I’ve lived my life by it, but it’s my past that cuts the deepest.”
